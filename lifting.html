Program Notes

  • • RPE Chart: Rate of Perceived Exertion - how hard the set feels (7 = 3 reps in reserve)
  • • Progressive Overload: Increase weight each week during 3-week build
  • • Week 4 Deload: Use same weight as week 1, fewer sets
  • • Rest Periods: As needed for main lifts, 1-3 min for accessories, 60-90s for smaller movements
  • • Form Priority: Film your main lifts for form review
